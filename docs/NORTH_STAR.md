# North Star

## Vision

Daily Progress Tracker is the personal productivity system that helps you achieve your goals through consistent daily tracking and AI-powered insights.

## Mission

To provide a beautiful, intuitive interface for tracking daily progress, visualizing achievements, and maintaining momentum toward your goals.

## Core Values

1. **Simplicity** - Easy to use daily
2. **Visibility** - Clear progress visualization
3. **Intelligence** - AI-powered insights
4. **Privacy** - Your data stays yours
